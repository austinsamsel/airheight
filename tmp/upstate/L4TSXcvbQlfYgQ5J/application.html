Application

Up State is a specific solution best suited for small websites for small to medium sized organizations. This application helps us make sure your project is a good fit.

Once you submit your application, please allow us 24 hours to review your application. The next step in the application process is a short phone interview to discuss your project in depth. And if your project isn't a fit? No worries - we'll send you a few suggestions and resources to help you better proceed.
